About Us

W&C was developed by a group of international scholars who are passionate and committed to helping students achieve higher education. Our experience drives us to inspire talented individuals to conquer the world of bright ideas. Studying in the United States has tremendous opportunities and we want our clients to experience them. However, as international students ourselves we understand every obstacle international students face before, during and after the admission process. W&C was created to help students in emerging economies to fulfill their own dreams.
